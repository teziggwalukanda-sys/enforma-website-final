Common questions

What people ask us.

How long does a project take?

▼

A single brand promo or event recap is 1–2 weeks from brief to delivery. A full content system takes 4–6 weeks to set up, then rolls monthly.

We've never done video before. Is that a problem?

▼

Not at all. We build the brief, brand direction, and production process together from scratch. No bad habits to undo.

Do you just do video, or is there more?

▼

Video is the entry point for most clients, but we cover creative direction, brand strategy, website design and build, content systems, and operations backend.

What does it cost?

▼

Every project is scoped individually. No fixed packages — we put together a proposal once we understand what you need. Book a call and we'll figure it out.
