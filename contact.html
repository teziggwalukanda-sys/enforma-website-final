Common questions

What people ask us.

How long does a project take?

▼

A single brand promo or event recap is typically 1–2 weeks from brief to delivery. A full content system with ongoing production takes 4–6 weeks to set up, then rolls monthly. We scope everything in the first conversation.

We've never done video before. Is that a problem?

▼

Not at all. It's actually easier. We come in without having to undo anything. We build the brief, the brand direction, and the production process together from the ground up.

Do you just do video, or is there more to it?

▼

Video is the entry point for most clients, but we cover the full picture. Creative direction, brand strategy, website design and build, content systems, and operations backend. We go as deep as you need.

What does it cost?

▼

Every project is scoped to the client. No fixed packages because no two businesses have the same needs. Once we understand what you're working with, we put together a proposal that makes sense for your situation. Book a call and we'll figure it out.

Who's my point of contact?

▼

You work directly with the team that built your project. No account managers, no handoffs. If something needs to change, you talk to the person who made it.
