Common questions

What clients ask us.

How long does implementation actually take?

▼

Depends on complexity. Simple email routing: 2 weeks. Full Salesforce + multi-channel: 4-6 weeks. We scope in week one. Most clients are live and seeing results within 30 days.

What if we outgrow this or need to change direction?

▼

Systems scale with you. We've grown clients from 5 to 50+ people without ripping out infrastructure. If priorities shift, we adapt. That's why we do monthly strategy calls.

Do you work with complex compliance needs?

▼

Yes. We handle data security seriously—NDA first, data stays in your infrastructure, encryption standard. Details depend on your specific setup. Discuss in discovery call.

How much customization? Are we locked into a template?

▼

Zero templates. We build your workflow from scratch based on your process. If you have a weird 7-step follow-up, we automate that exact flow. Customization is the default.

Who's my point of contact?

▼

You get assigned an account lead. Changes and support requests go to them. For strategy calls, you talk to the team that built it. No getting lost in support tickets.

Can we cancel if this doesn't work out?

▼

No long-term lock-in. We're confident in our work. If it's not delivering results, we refund setup and continue optimizing on retainer until it does. You never feel trapped.

Why aren't your fees bloated like other agencies?

▼

We charge for results, not hours. No account managers, no unnecessary layers, no phantom retainers. You get the team that built your system, solving your actual problems—not paying for overhead.

What does this actually cost?

▼

Depends on scope. Setup fee ranges $3-8k (strategy, build, launch). Monthly retainer $2-5k (monitoring, optimization, support). We quote after discovery call when we know the real scope.
